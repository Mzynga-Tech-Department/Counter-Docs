## Dashboards

Dashboards in counter are the marketplaces where the information about the ethereum that is being sold and the exchange rates are displayed.

All the information form fill in [How to sell](/HowToSell.md) steps accumulate to create a 
[dashboard](pages/dashboardGeneration/dashboard.md)
It is the responsibility of the seller who is putting up the dashboard to manage them.
By managing them we mean :- 
Reloading the Ethereum once the treasury runs dry
Renewing dashboards once they expire - A dashbaord expires after two weeks or after 72 hours without being toped up(loaded with ETH).

A dashboard must exist within a specific block in a specific chain.
This mechanism enables safe and secure transactions
RPC is provided by https://api.pocket.network

### Managing dashboard

Managing a dashboard can be done from [Edit Dashboard page](pages/dashboardGeneration/editDashboard.md)
